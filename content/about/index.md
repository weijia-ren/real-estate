## Our Story

Summit Realty Group was founded with a simple belief: real estate is about people, not just properties. What started as a small brokerage has grown into a full-service real estate firm serving buyers, sellers, investors, and property owners across the Austin metro area.

Over the past 15 years, we've helped hundreds of families find their dream homes, guided investors to build wealth through strategic acquisitions, and managed a growing portfolio of rental properties with care and professionalism.

## Our Mission

To provide exceptional, client-first real estate services that build lasting relationships and lasting wealth. We believe in transparency, integrity, and going the extra mile — every time.

## What Sets Us Apart

- **Local Expertise**: Our team lives and works in the communities we serve. We know every neighborhood, school district, and market trend.
- **Full-Service Approach**: From buying and selling to investing and managing, we offer everything under one roof.
- **Client-First Philosophy**: We measure our success by your success. That's why over 80% of our business comes from referrals and repeat clients.
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: We combine market intuition with rigorous data analysis to help you make informed choices.
