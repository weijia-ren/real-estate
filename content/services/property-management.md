## Hassle-Free Property Management

Owning rental property should build your wealth, not consume your weekends. Summit Realty Group provides comprehensive property management services that protect your investment and keep your tenants happy — so you don't have to.

### What We Handle

#### Tenant Management
- **Screening**: Thorough background checks, credit reports, income verification, and reference calls
- **Leasing**: Professional marketing, showings, and lease execution
- **Communication**: We're your tenants' primary point of contact — 24/7 emergency support included
- **Move-in / Move-out**: Detailed inspections and smooth transitions

#### Maintenance & Repairs
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Scheduled inspections and upkeep to avoid costly surprises
- **Repair Coordination**: Our network of vetted, licensed contractors handles repairs promptly and at competitive rates
- **Emergency Response**: After-hours emergency line for urgent issues
- **Capital Improvements**: We advise on strategic upgrades that increase property value and rental income

#### Financial Reporting
- **Monthly Statements**: Clear, detailed reports showing income, expenses, and net cash flow
- **Annual Summaries**: Tax-ready documentation including 1099s and expense breakdowns
- **Rent Collection**: Automated online rent collection with consistent follow-up on late payments
- **Budget Planning**: Proactive budgeting for maintenance reserves and capital expenditures

### Our Management Fees

We keep it simple and transparent:

- **Residential Management**: 8–10% of monthly collected rent
- **Leasing Fee**: One month's rent for new tenant placement
- **No hidden fees**: No markup on maintenance, no surprise charges

### By the Numbers

- 98% average occupancy rate across managed properties
- 48-hour average maintenance response time
- 15-day average time to fill vacancies
- $0 eviction cost to owners (we handle the process)
